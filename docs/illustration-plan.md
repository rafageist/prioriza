# Illustration Plan

The book benefits from conceptual diagrams where the method is clearer
visually than in prose: leveling, ties, progressive refinement, human
explanation, continuous form, and event-driven replanning.

Illustrations should be conceptual, not decorative. Each figure should help
explain an operation or methodological tension: why a tie appears, why a
recalculation is needed, why a result can contradict intuition, or how a new
question refines a decision.

Versioned conceptual figures currently live in `figures/`:

- `refinamiento-progresivo.svg`
- `explicacion-humana.svg`
- `replanificacion-evento.svg`
- `desempate-azar.svg`
- `forma-continua-tramos.svg`

PNG derivatives with the same base names are committed for book rendering on
local systems that do not provide `rsvg-convert`.

Future illustration work should keep source assets versionable and avoid
embedding opaque extracted media unless they are necessary historical evidence.
